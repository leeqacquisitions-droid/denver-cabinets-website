Multi-Unit & Builders

Estate Solutions is a trusted subcontracting partner for general contractors, developers, and property managers throughout the Denver metro area. We specialize in multi-unit residential cabinet installations—apartments, townhomes, condos, and commercial properties—delivering consistent quality, predictable timelines, and clean job sites.

Scheduling & Coordination

We work closely with project managers to align cabinet installs with your overall construction schedule. Lead times are confirmed upfront, and we batch-install units efficiently to minimize per-unit costs. We maintain open communication with on-site superintendents and adapt to trade sequencing as needed.

Quality Control Checklist

  • Pre-install verification: Wall/floor prep, plumbing/electrical rough-ins complete
  • Level, plumb, and secure mounting per manufacturer specs
  • Hardware installed and tested (hinges, drawer slides, soft-close)
  • Final walkthrough with project manager or owner rep
  • Punch-list correction within 48 hours of notice

Insurance & COI

Estate Solutions carries current general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We provide Certificates of Insurance (COI) to general contractors and property owners upon request. Proof of licensing is available for permit and compliance verification.
