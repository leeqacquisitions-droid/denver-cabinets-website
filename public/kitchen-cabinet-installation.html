Kitchen Cabinet Installation

Estate Solutions provides professional installation of pre-manufactured kitchen cabinets throughout the Denver metro area. We serve general contractors, property managers, and homeowners with reliable, on-schedule installations for remodels and new construction.

What's Included

  • Complete cabinet assembly and installation
  • Level, plumb, and secure mounting to walls and floors
  • Hardware installation (hinges, pulls, soft-close mechanisms)
  • Crown molding and trim work where applicable
  • Optional demo and haul-away of existing cabinets
  • Clean job site with daily cleanup

Typical Timeline

Most kitchen installs are completed in 4–6 days depending on kitchen size, cabinet complexity, and material lead times. We schedule installations to minimize disruption and keep your project on track. Multi-phase remodels are coordinated with other trades.

What We Need From You

  • Layout drawings or cabinet list: Manufacturer specs, dimensions, and placement
  • Schedule access: Clear dates for delivery and installation start
  • Site prep: Walls patched, painted, and ready; floors protected or finished
  • Coordination: Plumbing and electrical rough-ins completed before install
