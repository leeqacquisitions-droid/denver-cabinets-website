Water-Damage Cabinet Repair

Estate Solutions specializes in fast, cost-effective repair or replacement of water-damaged cabinets in the Denver metro area. Whether from a plumbing leak, appliance failure, or flood event, we'll assess the damage and restore your kitchen, bathroom, or utility cabinets to full function.

Signs of Water Damage

  • Swollen, warped, or delaminating cabinet doors and drawer fronts
  • Soft or discolored cabinet box floors, especially under sinks
  • Peeling veneer or finish bubbling on faces and edges
  • Mold or mildew odor inside cabinet boxes
  • Rust or corrosion on hinges, drawer slides, or mounting hardware

Repair vs. Replace

After an on-site inspection, we'll provide an honest assessment. Minor damage to door faces or isolated panels may be repairable. Extensive water intrusion into MDF or particleboard boxes typically requires full replacement. We'll match your existing style, finish, and hardware whenever possible.

Finish Matching Notes

For partial replacements, we work with pre-manufactured cabinet suppliers to source exact or close matches to your current finish. If an exact match isn't available, we can refinish adjacent units or provide color samples for approval. Finish variability depends on wood species, stain lot, and age of existing cabinets.
